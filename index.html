Non-linear Dynamics & Chaos

Dynamics is a mathematical philosophy concerned with how change occurs. A change is non-linear, in a mathematical sense, when it cannot be described by a numeric constant or a proportional variable. Chaos is the belief that mathematically defined rules can generate unpredicted, and unpredicatable, results.

x′ = cos(x) x′ = sin(x)

Each equation above can be read as: "x prime = cosine of x"

The prime symbol (′) indicates the derivative value of the associated variable. In other words, x prime is the derivative value of x.
